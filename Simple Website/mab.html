About Mabinay Spring

Mabinay Spring is a natural hot spring located in the beautiful municipality of Mabinay in Negros Oriental, Philippines. Known for its crystal-clear, mineral-rich waters, this natural wonder has been a destination for wellness seekers and nature lovers for generations.

The spring water contains beneficial minerals and maintains a warm temperature year-round, making it an ideal location for relaxation and rejuvenation. Whether you're seeking healing, relaxation, or simply a connection with nature, Mabinay Spring offers an unforgettable experience.
